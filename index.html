Welcome to Dragon Valley!

Aim of the game

In 'Dragon Valley', you control a majestic dragon with the aim of flying as far through the valley as possible, but making sure to dodge the deadly branches and vines. Dragons are slow starters so as you manage to make you way further, your speed will increase so do be careful!
How to play

The player will use his/her keyboard to play, with the W A D input keys to fly up, left and right (respectively). Be sure to log in to see who can manage to get the highest score out of you and your friends!
